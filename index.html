Welcome to House of Nodes

Your Place, Your Business, Your Future

Overview of House of Nodes

House of Nodes is a cooperative designed to empower individuals with skills but facing financial difficulties. Our members contribute their work and invoice through the cooperative, ensuring they operate legally and sustainably.

How It Works

Members invoice through House of Nodes, which retains a 5% administrative fee.

The cooperative manages accounting to ensure compliance and ease of operations.

Members run their own business areas, marketing under their own brands while leveraging the cooperative's network.

Benefits for Members

Community & Networking

Connect with skilled individuals, share expertise, and collaborate on projects.

Cross-Selling Opportunities

Gain access to customers from fellow members and expand your business reach.

Invoicing Support

Legally invoice for services and products with full administrative support.

Shared Workspaces

Use House of Nodes premises and facilities for your business needs.

Housing Assistance

Access homes under a rent-to-own principle, securing long-term stability.

Who We Help

Our typical members are skilled professionals experiencing financial difficulties. Whether you're a freelancer, an entrepreneur, or someone seeking financial independence, House of Nodes provides the structure and support needed to succeed.
